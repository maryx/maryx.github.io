Taste of Chemistry

The Taste of Chemistry program is a program for high school science teachers and covers taste and food science, so that the teachers can use the materials in their labs. Another component is a survey aimed at high school students that includes tasting various foods. Pilot data for 50 participants have been entered in the database and can be compared. The site was made with mySQL, PHP, and some JavaScript.

Pretty Alarm Clock!

This is an alarm clock whose background changes color based on the time, and plays the Hamster Dance video as the alarm. The goals for this unfinished project include allowing the user to choose the song, and changing the drop-down menu to something more ergonomic. This is made with JavaScript.

Evicted (Game)

You are an unhappy person who has been kicked out of your house. You start off with certain health, social, comfort, hunger, and money levels, which fluctuate depending on where you go in the world. The goal is to not die. The game is set in the US, using the Yelp database for locations. Working or eating/resting/visiting a location changes your stats, depending on the real-life weather or the reviews/price of the place (based on the Yelp reviews and data). This was made in the Databases class (CIS330) in a group of 3, and uses mySQL, PHP, and JavaScript.
